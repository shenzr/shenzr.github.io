Biography

I am now a postdoctoral fellow working with Prof. Patrick P. C. Lee in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. I obtained my B.S. degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2010, and Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Tsinghua University (supervised by Prof. Jiwu Shu) in 2016.

My current research interests include designing and building secure and dependable storage systems.
